ABOUT
US.

Terra Exports is committed to distributing the freshest fruits and vegetables while providing strategic market intelligence to our valuable partners. We have a fast-paced, entrepreneurial, young and innovative company culture that is reinventing international fruit trade.

The key to our success in this business is the close and personal multi-cultural approach we maintain with our customers and our constant search for new frontiers. Our company has a global presence, shipping our products from and to 65+ countries around the world. We are consistently expanding by pioneering new fruits and vegetables into different markets globally.

Terra Exports has been featured 4 times in INC 5000's annual list of Fastest Growing Companies in the US, thanks to our global partners that continuously put their trust in our diverse team.
